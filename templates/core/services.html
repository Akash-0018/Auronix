Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my services

How long does it take to complete a website?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity of the project. A basic website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects with custom functionality may take 5-6 weeks or more. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements.

Do you provide hosting services?

I don't provide hosting directly, but I can recommend reliable hosting providers based on your needs and assist with the setup process to ensure your website is properly deployed and configured.

What is your payment structure?

I typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. For larger projects, we can establish a milestone-based payment schedule. All payment terms will be clearly outlined in our contract.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All websites I develop are fully responsive and mobile-friendly, ensuring an optimal viewing experience across all devices, from desktop computers to smartphones and tablets.

Can you help with existing websites?

Yes, I offer services for updating, redesigning, and improving existing websites. Whether you need minor updates or a complete overhaul, I can help enhance your current online presence.
